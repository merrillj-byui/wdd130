Sitka Nature Adventure Site Plan

Joe Merrill

WDD 130-B1

Overview

Purpose

To introduce the tourist, the casual explorer, and the curious adventurer to points of interest at Sitka, Alaska. From a wild bear refuge, to a raptor rehabilitation site, to a science museum and fish hatchery, there are a variety of points of interest for the nature lover. Or, from fishing adventures, to whale watching, to nature hikes, there are a variety of ways to explore the wild around Sitka.

Audience

Those who are interested in learning about and visiting interesting sites in Sitka, Alaska.
